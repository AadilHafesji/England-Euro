Manchester United

Manchester United is an English football club based in Old Trafford, Greater Manchester. They compete in the English Premier League winning the competition 20 times. The club is nicknamed "The Red Devils".

Along with winning the Premier League 20 times, Manchester United have won 12 FA Cups, 5 League Cups (Carabao Cup), 21 Community Shields, 3 UEFA Champions Leagues, 1 UEFA Europa Leagues, 1 UEFA Cup Winners' Cup, 1 UEFA Super Cup, 1 Intercontinental Cup and 1 FIFA Club World Cup.
